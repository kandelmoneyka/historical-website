Rupandehi

Rupandehi District, a part of Lumbini Province, is one of the seventy-seven districts of Nepal and covers an area of 1,360 kmÂ². The district headquarter is Bhairahawa. As per the national census 2011, the population of Rupandehi was 880,196. Tinau River Kothi River,Sukhaira River,Bagela River,Rohini River,Kanchan River are the main rivers of Rupandehi District. The main languages spoken in the district are Magar,Tharu, Nepali etc.The district is surrounded by Palpa on the north, India on the south, Kapilvastu on the west and Nawalparasi on the east.
