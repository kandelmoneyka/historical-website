Rukum

RukumRukum is a district located in the western part of Nepal. It is part of the Rapti Province and is known for its mountainous terrain, diverse flora and fauna, and unique cultural heritage. The district covers an area of 2,877 square kilometers and has a population of approximately 207,290 people according to the 2021 census. Rukum is divided into nine rural municipalities and one urban municipality, with its district headquarters in Musikot. The district is bordered by Dolpa and Jajarkot to the north, Salyan to the east, Rolpa to the south, and Pyuthan to the west.
