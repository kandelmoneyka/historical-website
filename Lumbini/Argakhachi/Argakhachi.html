Argakhachi

Arghakhanchi a isdistrict in the Lumbini Province of Nepal. It is located in the western part of Nepal and covers an area of 1,193 square kilometers. The district is bordered by Gulmi District to the east, Palpa District to the west, Kapilvastu District to the south, and Baglung District to the north. The district's headquarters is in Sandhikharka, and the major rivers in the district are the Tinau, Khahare, and Bangi Khola. The district is known for its natural beauty, historical and cultural heritage, and agricultural products such as coffee and oranges.
